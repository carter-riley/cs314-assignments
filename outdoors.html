The Great Outdoors

What I love to do

Throughout my life as I grew up, I lived near the forest. My daily goal was to explore a new part of the pine forest near my house. I have always loved the outdoors whether I was going to a 15 mile run or a 30 mile hike. As I have grown up, I have had less and less time to explore the outdoors, but it is still my favorite activity for my free time. Below I have listed some of the places I want to go.
